📖 About Project

Next-Generation Emotion Intelligence

The Generalizable Emotion Classifier is a cutting-edge research project that leverages deep learning to understand human emotions from text with unprecedented granularity.

Built on the GoEmotions dataset with 58,000+ annotated Reddit comments, our system combines traditional machine learning with state-of-the-art transformer models to achieve superior accuracy in detecting 27 distinct emotions.
